About Luminary Books

Our story, mission, and the people behind the pages

Our Story

Luminary Books began in 2010 with a simple premise: to create a sanctuary for book lovers and bibliophiles. What started as a small corner bookshop in the heart of New York's Literary District has grown into a beloved institution dedicated to the art of storytelling.

Our founder, Elizabeth Blackwell, started Luminary Books after a lifetime of collecting rare editions and first prints. Her vision was to create not just a bookstore, but a community hub where literature could be celebrated, discussed, and shared across generations.

Our Mission

At Luminary Books, we believe that a good book has the power to transform lives. Our mission is to connect readers with stories that inspire, challenge, and transport them to new worlds.

Curation

We meticulously select each title in our collection, ensuring a diverse range of voices and perspectives.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging through book clubs, author events, and literary discussions.

Expertise

Our staff are passionate readers with deep knowledge across genres, ready to help you find your next favorite book.
